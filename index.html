🧠 Headbands

Hold it to your forehead. No peeking! 😄
How to play:
1. Turn your phone sideways (landscape) and hold it against your forehead (screen facing your friends).
2. They give you clues — guess the word!
3. Each word lasts 20 seconds. When time's up, flip the phone down and back up for the next word! ⏱️
On iPhone, tap “Allow” for Motion & Orientation access. Tip: add to your Home Screen for full-screen play.
